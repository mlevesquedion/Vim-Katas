### Combine and conquer

Use this text for practicing:

```text
This is some text, I hope you like it.
  one more
 and more
```

Do the following exercises on it:

`2cw` - change 2 words  
`2dw` - delete 2 words  
`2yw` - yank 2 words into register  
`g~`  - swap case  
`gu`  - make selection all lower case  
`gU`  - make selection all UPPER case  
`>`   - shift right  
`<`   - shift left  
`=`   - auto indent
